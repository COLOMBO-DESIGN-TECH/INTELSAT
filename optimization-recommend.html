OPTIMIZATION RECOMMENDATIONS

Away from Interference:
Keep the router away from large metal objects, thick walls, and other electronic devices like microwaves or cordless phones.

Clear Sky View:
Place the dish in an open area with no obstructions (trees, buildings, or power lines).

Central Location:
Position the router in the middle of your home for even Wi-Fi coverage.

Elevated Position:
Place it on a shelf or mount it higher to minimize signal obstruction.
